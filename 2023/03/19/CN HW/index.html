LEC2 神经生理学基础

3210102492 陈雪阳

1. . 神经元按形态、功能和突触来分类，分别可以分成哪几类、名称是什么？

Solutions:

Neurons Classification By Morphology:

  1. unipolar neurons

    single nerve fiber extends from cell body then branching into one axon(轴突) and one dendrite(树突).

  2. bipolar neurons

    one axon and one dendrite from cell body; found in special sensory areas (eyes, ears, nose).

  3. multipolar neurons

    one axon and many dendrites from cell body; carrying information in cerebral cortex,CNS.

Neurons Classification By Function:

  1. sensory(感觉) neurons
  2. motor(运动) neurons
  3. inter(中间)neurons

Neurons Classification By Synapses:

  1. excitatory(兴奋) neurons
  2. inhibitory(抑制) neurons

2.简述神经元的主要组成部分及各部分功能，并用图表示。

Solutions：

Most neurons have the four functional regions，dendrites, soma, axon and axon-end.

Dendrites: Input component, produces graded local signals.

Soma: The integrative zone, makes the decision to generate an AP.

Axon:Conductive component, propagates an all-or-none AP .

Axon-end:Output component, releases neurotransmitter, interact with other neurons.

3.神经元是通过什么方式传递信息，该方式传递信息的主要特点是什么？

Solutions：

Signaling of all neurons is organized in the same way, via Action Potentials. It’s directional, All-or-nothing and Non-degrading.

4.离子通道按门控方式有哪几类？并举出两种电压门控的离子通道。

Solutions：

There are four types of ion channels:

  • Voltage-gating （电压门控）
  • Phosphorylation-gating（磷酸化门控）
  • Ligand-gating （配合基门控）
  • Stretch/pressure-gating （机械门控）

For instance, Sodium (Na+Na^+Na+) channels, Calcium (Ca2+Ca^{2+}Ca2+) channels and Potassium (K+K^+K+) channels are VGICs.

5. 从脑科学角度，浅谈研究大脑的不同层次或尺度。

Solutions：

Neuroscience, also known as brain science, is the study of the structure and function of the brain which is highly interdisciplinary.

  • Molecular and cellular neuroscience: the study of molecules such as ion channels, neurotransmitters, proteins, etc. and cells such as various types of neurons, glial cells, etc. in the nervous system. Also the study of neurological diseases and the development of the nervous system .

  • Systems and Loop Neuroscience: the study of neural loop mechanisms of cognitive functions such as perception, choice, learning and memory in experimental animals by invasive methods.

  • Cognitive and behavioral neuroscience: the study of the neural mechanisms underlying higher cognitive functions such as perception, choice, learning and memory, language, emotion, and consciousness in humans by non-invasive methods.

  • Evolutionary and comparative neuroscience: The study of the differences and evolution of cognitive systems through cross-species comparisons.

  • Theoretical and computational neuroscience: the study of analysis and modelling of neural systems and cognitive processes using mathematical tools with the aim of understanding the brain.

    Considered to be the leading discipline to strong artificial intelligence.
